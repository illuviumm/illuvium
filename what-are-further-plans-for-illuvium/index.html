    What are further plans for Illuvium

    Illuvium has sold digital land plots that brought a huge stockpile of capital worth $72 million for the project.

    Start project

    Upcoming role-playing game, Illuvium has recently sold digital land plots’ worth $72 million during a sale. The sale of digital land began on Thursday and ultimately had seen the sale of upto 20,000 digital land plots. The sale ended on Sunday when the plots were sold to investors and potential players. Digital land of Illuvium run on a prominent layer two scaling solution of network Immutable X. This scaling solution enables faster, cheaper, and energy-efficient.

    What next?

    Illuvium is set to launch later this year on Windows and Mac computers. Illuvium will have a game world that will feature about 100,000 land plots ultimately whose holders will receive so many various in-game benefits’ accessibility. These benefits include extracting in-game fuel that can be further sold to other players for a potential profit.

    There were 29 out of initially offered 20,000 plots that developers have retained for future giveaways. In comparison, additional two more plots from Tier 5, which are the most premium in the game, will be auctioned using a standard auction format.

    For selling:

    Illuvium used the auction format that the Dutch use, where each plot’s price decreases gradually during the sale of plots belonging to each batch. Prices of these digital land depending upon their Tier, vary accordingly where Tier 1 plots start at 2 units that costs approximately $3,700, Tier 2 plots for 6 units, which is ($11,100), and plots from Tier 3 are selling at 20 units, $37,000 and Tier 4 plots have been available for 80 units that is $148,000, as per current price.
